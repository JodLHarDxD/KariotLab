Questions, annotated.

How is this different from hiring an agency?+
An agency takes 3–6 months and costs $80k–$300k+ per cycle, then disappears. Lumis takes hours, costs $49–$249/mo, and stays with your brand as it evolves. The work is informed by the same award archive senior agency creatives reference — the difference is that you stay in the room for every decision.
Do I own the output?+
Yes, fully. Every wordmark, palette, type spec, voice rule, and asset Lumis generates is yours, free and clear, exportable in W3C design tokens, Figma, code, or print-ready. Cancel any time, take everything with you.
What does "living system" actually mean?+
Your brand isn't frozen at launch. As you raise, pivot, hire, or expand, Lumis recommends thoughtful updates — with provenance, versioning, and equity carried forward. You approve every change. Roll back anything. Watch the system learn alongside the company.
What if my brand is already established?+
Scale plan includes custom training on your existing brand archive — we encode what already works, then help you evolve it without breaking equity. Many of our most loved cases are not first-time brand builds; they are renovations done with respect.
Can I see real work done with Lumis?+
142 systems shipped to date, archived in /work. North Capital, Atelier Vesta, Casa Lume, Field Notes, Selected, Hum, Brick, Slow, Vesper, Seed — every system live, evolving, and signed.
What if I'm not technical?+
Most of our founders aren't. Lumis speaks plain language, asks the right questions, and produces a system you can hand to a developer or designer who is — or use directly through our Figma + code exports.
What happens if I cancel?+
Cancel any time. Your brand system is yours forever — we never claw back assets. You lose access to continuous updates and the living system tools, but everything you've already exported stays usable. Re-subscribe and we pick up where we stopped.
Do you work with agencies or internal teams?+
Yes, both. Several Series-B teams use Lumis as their internal brand operating system, run by their existing in-house designers. Several agencies use Lumis to do strategic exploration faster, then layer their own craft on top. The Scale plan supports either workflow.
