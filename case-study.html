// The brief

North arrived after a Series A with a problem most fintechs share: their identity belonged to "financial software" generally, not to North specifically. The category was crowded with confident blues and exclamation-point gradients.

They wanted to feel quiet, legible, and seriously useful — the way a well-cut suit feels in a room of t-shirts.

Lumis took the brief at 9:14am. By 12:40pm, North had a strategic foothold, a typographic system, and a working palette that argued for the position. The full 18-surface system was production-ready inside three weeks.

It's the first time we’ve had a brand that understands what we mean when we don’t finish a sentence.

Sasha Reyes   ·   CEO, North Capital
